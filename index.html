📱Interrupt Recall Test — Memorize 5 digits, get exposed to a live social feed for 20 seconds, then recall. The most realistic test.

Research Notes
🔬Gloria Mark (UC Irvine): it takes an average of 23 minutes to fully re-focus after a single digital interruption — not seconds.
📉Heavy social media users score 15–20% lower on standardized working memory tasks compared to low-use peers across multiple studies.
🛌Phone use within 30 minutes of waking disrupts overnight memory consolidation, directly reducing daily recall efficiency.
💪A 1-week phone-free morning routine measurably improves attention span and verbal memory scores in controlled trials.
